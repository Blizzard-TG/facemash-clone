About PictureMash

Our Mission: To create an engaging and fair platform where users can vote on the most appealing image in a pair while learning how crowd-based decision making works.

How it works: Users vote anonymously on image pairs. Logged-in users may upload their own image pairs (up to 5 per 24 hours). Admins moderate content and maintain fairness by controlling score updates and content visibility.

Security & Privacy: PictureMash ensures all user data is protected and handles sensitive information in compliance with GDPR and HIPAA regulations.

Our Team: PictureMash was developed by a team of developers, designers, and community enthusiasts who care about transparency, fairness, and creativity.
