Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to PictureMash. We are committed to protecting your personal data and ensuring your privacy in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA).

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal identifiers (name, email, age, gender, nationality)
  • Uploaded image data
  • Voting history and IP addresses
  • Login credentials (securely encrypted)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To allow secure voting and leaderboard updates
  • To authenticate user and admin accounts
  • To improve our service and analyze anonymous usage data

4. Data Security

We use SSL encryption and token-based authentication. Sensitive data is hashed and stored securely. Admin privileges are strictly managed.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

  • You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • You may request data export or restriction of processing

6. HIPAA Compliance

Any health-related data (if ever applicable) is handled following HIPAA regulations, including physical, technical, and administrative safeguards.

7. Data Retention

Your data is retained only for as long as necessary to serve the purpose for which it was collected. Inactive accounts may be deleted after 12 months of inactivity.

8. Contact

If you have questions or concerns, please contact us at support@picturemash.com.
